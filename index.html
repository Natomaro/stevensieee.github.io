Our Mission

We are the Stevens Institute of Technology branch of IEEE. We enable students to pursue interests in the field of Electrical and Computer Engineering outside of the classroom and provide opportunities for professional development.

We offer our members the resources to further their skills and careers in the scope of Electrical and Computer Engineering. We are always open to new ideas and new people to collaborate with.

Personal Growth

We aim to allow students to grow their knowledge and better themselves in the fields of Electrical and Computer Engineering through interactive events such as:

  • The IEEE Micromouse Competiton
  • Yearly IEEE Hackathon
  • Skill Developing workshops
  • Lectures by notable and distinguished professors in our community

Technology

We help students innovate and invent new technology. Students have access to resources to persue their own projects and hobbies.

  • We have an arsenal of Raspberry Pis and Arduinos
  • Our members are always willing to share their knowledge on their field sof study
  • IEEE provides access to large libraries of online resources such as scientific journals and online tutorials

Community

Our community is welcoming and full of people willing to learn and grow together. IEEE is a global society with mebers all over the world, that we are always happy to meet and work with.

  • We are run by students for students
  • We organize and hold events that the entire Stevens community can participate in
  • Collaboration is encouraged, and a key part of how we work
